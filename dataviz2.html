Project
ONLINE REPORT Analysis of a specific topic through Digital Methods. The topic was tourism on Social Media. The research aimed to investigate the impact of the social media (in particular Instagram) on the tourism situation in the world. The research analysed social media platforms (Instagram and TripAdvisor) in order to grasp the variety of tourism categories, to comprehend how a photographic trend spreads across different platforms and to single out specific types of tourism and investigate them thoroughly.

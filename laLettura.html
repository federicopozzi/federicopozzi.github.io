Project
CANCELLAZIONE and TRAMA are a visual translation in the form of a visualisation of data from analyses carried out on the works of Italo Calvino, these two documents in particular are a print translation of some virtual visualisations of Atlante Calvino . The visualisations were produced for publication in the monthly insert of corriere della sera: laLettura.
Role
Concept, Dataset building and refining, Rawgraphs, Web design, Group coordination.
Team
Sara Ferrini, Marco Valli, Valentina Pallacci, Federico Pozzi.
